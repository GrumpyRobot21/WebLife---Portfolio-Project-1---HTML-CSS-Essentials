Digital Legacy Management Specialists.

In today's online world, we are connected to the internet in many different ways. Controlling our accounts, social media, photos, documents, videos and email is almost a full time job. Imagine what would happen to it if you were no longer around to manage it?

At 'WebLife' we provide Digital Legacy Management solutions that help take care of your “Web life” from now and after you're gone. We ensure a private, secure and discreet service to make sure your information is looked after when you're no longer around to manage it yourself.

Digital Wills

WebLife will integrate your social media and complete digital life into your last will and testament. WebLife's unique solution ensures peace of mind when it comes to your wishes being carried out by your executors.

Digital Estate Management

WebLife's Legacy Managers provide a full suite of estate planning tools coupled with robust solutions for managing all of your personal accounts securely.

Secure Storage Solutions

WebLife provides a fully secure, web-based, encrypted online repository for all of your digital assests. A comprehensive, discreet digital legacy management solution for the modern age.
